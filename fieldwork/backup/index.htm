Feedback

This site is designed to accompany the book Linguistic Field Work: A Practical Guide (Palgrave MacMillan, 2007). It contains the information that is likely to be highly datable (especially web links and information about audio equipment). It also contains many of the checklists given in the book.

If you are looking for a particular item referenced in the text of the book, check the Table of Contents list.

You may download and print copies any of the material on this site for your own use (e.g. to take to the field), however you may not republish any of the information on this site, nor may you distribute it to a third party. Please provide a link to the site instead. This material is all free, but if you find it useful, I strongly encourage you to buy a copy of the book - it'll be even more useful then! Clicking on the picture of the book's cover in the top left-hand corner of the site will take you to Palgrave Macmillan's site. The book is also available from the regular online sources, including Amazon.

I will update this site three times a year, with more frequent minor updates where necessary. Updates will be in mid-October, mid-February and mid-June.

Feedback is very welcome!

Materials Bleg:

Put your stimulus materials on the web! If you have created elicitation prompts or stimulus materials for your fieldwork, please think about making them available to others. I can host materials here or provide a link. Please send information to me using the feedback link.
